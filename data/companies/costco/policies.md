# Policies

## Membership Required
All purchases require a valid Costco membership. Gold Star ($65/yr) or Executive ($130/yr with 2% reward). The agent should verify the caller has an active membership. If not, offer to start signup.

## Pickup Time (Same-Day & Curbside)
- Standard same-day pickup: ready in 2-4 hours after order
- Express curbside pickup (limited items): 1 hour
- During peak times (Saturday 11am-2pm, Sunday 12pm-4pm): allow extra hour
- The agent should quote "ready in about 2 hours" for standard same-day orders

## Bulk Order Discount
Orders over $500 qualify for additional 2% Executive Member reward. Orders over $1500 may qualify for business delivery scheduling. Mention this to customers placing large bulk orders.

## Costco Rotisserie Chicken
Always $4.99, always 3 lbs, never on sale, available daily 10am-8pm. If asked, confirm price has not changed since 2009. Limit 4 per member per visit.

## Kirkland Signature Brand
Costco's premium private label. Generally 20-30% cheaper than national brand equivalents. The agent should proactively suggest Kirkland alternatives when relevant ("We have a Kirkland version for $X less").

## Return Policy
- Most items: Risk-free 100% satisfaction guarantee, returnable anytime
- Electronics (TVs, computers, cameras, phones): 90-day return window
- Diamonds over 1ct: requires Costco gemologist verification
- The agent should NOT promise specific return windows beyond these; defer to in-warehouse member services for edge cases

## Substitutions
For grocery delivery, Costco may substitute similar items. Customer can specify "no substitutions" or list acceptable swaps (e.g., "if no Kirkland organic eggs, sub Vital Farms"). The agent should ask preference for substitutions on perishables.

## Bulk Order Limits
Most items have purchase limits during high-demand periods (toilet paper, water, formula). The agent should check current limits before confirming large orders.

## Escalation to Human
Escalate to a human team member if:
- Customer has a complaint about product quality or service
- Customer wants to dispute a charge
- Customer asks about prescription, optical, or hearing aid services (require specialist)
- Customer wants to book Costco Travel (requires travel agent)
- Allergen questions for prepared foods (always escalate, never guess)
Say: "Let me connect you with a specialist — one moment." Then end gracefully.

## Hours
Warehouse open 10am-8:30pm M-F, 9:30am-6pm Sat, 10am-6pm Sun. Gas station opens 1 hour earlier and closes 30 min later. Pharmacy and Optical have separate hours.

## Same-Day Delivery via Instacart
Available for grocery items only. Delivery fee waived for Executive members on orders $35+. Tipping suggested but not required. Quoted time: 1-2 hours.

## Two-Day Shipping
Free for most non-grocery items on orders $75+. Tracked via Costco.com account. The agent should confirm shipping address before placing order.

## Price Protection
If item price drops within 30 days of purchase, Costco refunds the difference. Customer must bring receipt to member services. The agent can flag recent price changes proactively.

## Modifications
Order modifications allowed up to 1 hour after placement for pickup orders. Cancellations free up to 30 minutes before pickup time. The agent should confirm all items and quantities before finalizing.
