# Policies

## Pickup Time
Orders are typically ready in 10–15 minutes. During peak hours (7–9am, 12–1pm), allow up to 20 minutes. The agent should quote "about 10 minutes" for standard orders.

## Order Hold
Completed orders are held at the counter for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, the order may be released. Customers can call to request an extension.

## Loyalty Program
Every 10th drink is free. The agent should mention this when a customer places their order if they are a returning customer. Free drink applies to beverages only, not food.

## Substitutions
All dairy-based drinks can be made with oat milk, almond milk, or soy milk at no extra charge. Gluten-free bread substitution is $1.50 extra. The agent can offer substitutions proactively for customers who prefer dairy-free.

## Escalation to Human
If a customer asks about severe allergen concerns, has a complaint, wants to speak to a manager, or has a situation the agent can't handle, say: "Let me connect you with our team directly — one moment." Then end the call gracefully. Never guess about allergens — always escalate.

## Modifications
All drink modifications are noted on the order. Complex modifications (more than 3) may take slightly longer. The agent should confirm modifications before finalizing.
