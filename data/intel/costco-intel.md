## Company Brief

Costco is a membership-based retail and wholesale company operating through
an e-commerce platform and physical warehouses. It offers a wide variety
of products including groceries, electronics, and household goods, along
with various services. Members can access exclusive deals, bulk purchasing
options, and special savings.

## Voice Agent Talking Points

These are surfaced to the Vapi agent when relevant keywords appear in a
customer's request. Each is grounded in the intel above.

- **On "membership"**: Mention that Executive membership pays for itself
  with the 2% reward and Costco Travel pricing — competitor Sam's Club
  is the closest alternative but doesn't bundle travel the same way.

- **On "bulk" or "wholesale"**: Surface The Hog's tracked trend that
  bulk purchasing is up across the category; lean into Kirkland Signature
  as Costco's wholesale-priced private label (avg. 20-30% under national
  brands).

- **On "delivery" or "shipping"**: Same-day grocery delivery via
  Instacart is part of Costco's grocery-delivery-innovation play; for
  non-grocery, free 2-day shipping over $75.

- **On "compare" / "vs" / a competitor name**: Don't bash the competitor.
  Stay factual: Costco's edge is the curated assortment + Kirkland +
  Executive reward. If a customer mentions Sam's Club explicitly, note
  that Costco's pricing is generally comparable and the Kirkland line is
  consistently rated higher in third-party tests.

- **On "loyalty" / "rewards"**: Executive membership rewards 2% on most
  purchases (annual reward typically $400-600 for heavy households).
